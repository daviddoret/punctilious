proof-by-refutation-2
=========================

Definition
----------

*proof-by-refutation-2* is the :ref:`inference-rule`:

.. math::

    \left( \mathcal{H} assume \not \mathbf{P}, Inc\left(\mathcal{H}\right)  \right) \vdash \mathbf{P}
